Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Socialiser collects the following information to provide our location-based social discovery service:

  • Account Information: Email address and full name when you register
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates to show nearby trendy spots and verify check-ins
  • Photos: Profile pictures and social posts you choose to share
  • Activity Data: Spots you visit, badges earned, and social interactions
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Show you nearby trendy cafes, restaurants, and fitness spots
  • Verify your location for authentic check-ins
  • Enable social features like badges, achievements, and activity feeds
  • Send notifications about nearby trending spots
  • Improve our app experience and discover new features

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information:

  • With other users: Your profile name, posts, and activity (not your email or exact location)
  • For safety: If required by law or to protect user safety
  • Service providers: Trusted partners who help operate our app (under strict confidentiality)

4. Location Privacy

Your location is used to:

  • Show nearby spots within a reasonable distance
  • Verify you're actually at a location for check-ins
  • Send notifications about trending spots near you

We never share your exact GPS coordinates with other users or third parties.

5. Data Security

We protect your information using:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Protected database storage
  • Regular security updates

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Turn off location services (though this limits app functionality)
  • Opt out of notifications

7. Children's Privacy

Socialiser is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
